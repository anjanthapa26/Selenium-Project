Vasion, formerly PrinterLogic, is looking for a talented DevOps Engineer with Azure experience to join our growing team based in St. George, UT (available as fully-remote in the U.S.). The primary responsibility will be building, maintaining, troubleshooting, and automating cloud infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines in a microservice environment.

Key Responsibilities:
Build, operate, maintain, and automate global cloud infrastructure (Azure, AWS) for a microservice environment
Identify and solve infrastructure-related problems with infrastructure as code (IaC)
Support CI/CD pipelines (build, maintain, troubleshoot)
Improve operations by automating tasks and/or creating tools
Participate in 24/7 on-call rotation

Skills & Experience Required:
Strong understanding and hands-on experience with (Azure, AWS) in a production environment
Hands-on experience with Linux systems administration
Broad knowledge of infrastructure services including networking, DNS, load balancing, databases / MySQL, security, etc.
2+ years of experience in a cloud DevOps role

Skills & Experience Preferred:
Application Performance Monitoring and troubleshooting
Hands-on experience with container orchestration technologies such as Docker Swarm, Kubernetes
Hands-on experience with Jenkins, Datadog, Terraform, CloudFormation
Strong in one more or programming/scripting languages (Bash, Python, Go)
Excellent critical thinking, communication, presentation, and coding abilities
Bachelor's degree in, computer science, information systems, or equivalent work experience

Relevant Certifications:
Azure Administrator Associate (AZ-104)
Azure Solutions Architect Expert (AZ-305)
AWS Certified SysOps Administrator
AWS Certified Solutions Architect

About Vasion:
Vasion, formerly PrinterLogic, builds upon the company’s enterprise print management technology and the ECM/document management expertise the company gained through its acquisition of MaxxVault to enable organizations to digitize content and automate business processes to drive compliance, scalability, and accountability. With Vasion’s SaaS platform, businesses can capture analog and digital content, automate workflows, and securely manage content wherever it is stored. The company has been included multiple times on the Inc. 5000 and Deloitte Fast 500 lists of fastest growing companies in North America. For more information visit https://www.vasion.com.
